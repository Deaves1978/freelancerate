# FreelanceRate.io

**What should you *actually* charge?**

Most freelancers undercharge by 30–50%. [FreelanceRate.io](https://www.freelancerate.io/) is a free calculator that factors in taxes, lost benefits, business expenses, unbillable time, and profit margin to find your real minimum rate.

No signup. No tracking. Just math.

🔗 **[www.freelancerate.io](https://www.freelancerate.io/)**
